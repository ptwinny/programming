About Me

I studied literature and creative writing at the University of Texas at Austin. Later I studied education and learned to conduct research at the Univeristy of Texas at San Antonio and Teachers College, Columbia Univeristy. I learned HTML, CSS, and JavaScript using Harvard's CS50 online course and earned certifications in each language through freecodecamp.org.

For years I taught English at the high school level and the teaching of English at the graduate level. But I'm now transitioning into working in tech and writing both fiction and non-fiction. I'm knowledgeable in literature, tech, fitness, cooking, and education.

My writing can be found at thewhalebelly.com.
